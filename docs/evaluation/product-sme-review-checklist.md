# Product-SME review checklist

Use this checklist after the automated release harness passes and before a Product edition is
approved for publication. Review the rendered edition and open every cited source/evidence record.
Automated checks are necessary but do not replace this review.

## Mandatory stop conditions

Reject the edition without scoring it if any condition below is true:

- A cited source or evidence record cannot be opened, does not support the nearby claim, or is
  materially misquoted.
- The edition combines legacy and candidate pipeline/model versions without a clearly approved,
  evidence-level migration rule.
- A deadline, effective date, event date, publication date, or reporting window is materially wrong.
- A recommendation is addressed to a competitor or to HR, Marketing, Sales, or another function
  instead of our Product team.
- A future-looking action is already expired at the edition's as-of date.
- A claimed trend lacks at least two independent events and two independent sources.
- A single-source item is presented as decision-ready rather than as a labelled watch signal.
- The edition has fewer than three decision-ready insights but presents itself as a full monthly or
  quarterly report instead of an insufficient-evidence/watch brief.

## Scored review

Score each dimension from 1 to 5 using the anchor rubric below. Record one short evidence-based note
for every score below 5.

| Dimension | What the reviewer must verify | Score | Evidence / correction needed |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Vietnam offer relevance | The insight identifies a concrete Vietnam customer need, segment, proposition, benefit, pricing/fee, eligibility, servicing or distribution implication. Foreign-market material is not treated as Vietnam evidence. | /5 | |
| Regulation | The rule, regulator, jurisdiction, affected product/process, effective date and required Product response are correct. Regulation is separated from promotions, awards and company news. | /5 | |
| Regional transfer | A regional idea states what is transferable, what is not, and the Vietnam validation needed for demand, regulation, economics, operations and distribution. Unsupported copy-and-paste recommendations are absent. | /5 | |
| Temporal correctness | Publication, event, effective, expiry, deadline and forecast dates are distinct; the event belongs in the report window or is explicitly labelled as a continuing theme; recommended timing is still actionable. | /5 | |
| Coherence and pattern | Each insight has one clear thesis. Its evidence concerns the same event/pattern, comparisons use a meaningful baseline, and trend language is proportional to the number and independence of sources/events. | /5 | |
| Product actionability | “So what” names our Product decision, a concrete action/test, owner, time horizon, expected learning or decision criterion, and material evidence gap. Generic monitoring is absent. | /5 | |
| Citation quality | Every material factual claim resolves to evidence that entails it; source independence and conflicts are visible; citations are placed next to claims; no title-only item is used as substantive evidence. | /5 | |

## Score anchors

Use the same anchors for all seven dimensions:

- **5 — Publication-ready:** specific, correct, decision-useful and fully evidenced; no substantive
  edit is needed.
- **4 — Acceptable:** correct and useful with only minor wording, ordering or non-material context
  improvements.
- **3 — Needs revision:** directionally useful, but a material gap, ambiguity or weak comparison
  prevents confident Product use.
- **2 — Weak:** mostly descriptive, generic, poorly transferred or thinly evidenced; substantial
  rework is needed.
- **1 — Unsafe/irrelevant:** wrong, misleading, stale, incoherent, outside Product scope or
  unsupported.

## Acceptance rule

The edition is accepted only when:

1. no mandatory stop condition is present;
2. every dimension scores at least **4/5**;
3. the mean score is at least **4.0/5**; and
4. the reviewer records an explicit decision below.

A score must not be averaged upward to hide a weak dimension. Any dimension below 4 returns the
edition for revision and a complete re-review after correction.
