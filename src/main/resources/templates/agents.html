Desk 1 — Gather · Thu thập
Bring the market in — safely, once, and without waste.
Gather
Scout
Trinh sát
@scout
“I never leave the allowlist.”
Crawls 40+ whitelisted sources — HTTPS-only, SSRF-blocked, size-capped; reads JS-rendered sites through their own JSON APIs.
NO MODELTOOL: SafeFetcher
Gather
Librarian
Thủ thư
@librarian
“A duplicate never costs a model call.”
Spots duplicates before they cost money — normalized hash first, similarity second, AI only for genuine gray zones.
NO MODEL*TOOL: SHA-256 + Jaccard
Gather
Router
Điều phối
@router
“I read every story three times, then vote.”
3-vote self-consistency topic classification; routes each story to the departments it concerns. Disagreement goes to a human, never a guess.
MODELGUARD: 2/3 vote
Desk 2 — Understand · Phân tích
Turn articles into evidence, evidence into decisions.
Understand
Researcher
Nghiên cứu viên
@researcher
“I may only quote — never write.”
Extracts verbatim evidence spans; long documents are chunked with overlap so every character is read. Five kinds of dates, each grounded in its span.
MODELGUARD: verbatim gate
Understand
Connector
Người kết nối
@connector
“One article is never a trend.”
Clusters evidence across documents and months by company, product, event type and geography — corroboration counted in independent sources, structurally.
NO MODELTOOL: MarketEvent clusters
Understand
Analyst
Chuyên viên phân tích
@analyst
“What, pattern, so-what, now-what — with citations.”
Writes bilingual decision briefs from gated evidence only; every recommendation is owned by our Product team with a 30–90-day horizon and a stop criterion.
MODELGUARD: Desk 3
Desk 3 — Guard · Kiểm chứng
Nothing publishes because a model said so.
Guard
Editor
Biên tập viên
@editor
“Awards and PR fluff never make my report.”
Scores materiality against 7 Product intelligence questions; assigns DECISION_READY / WATCH / REJECT. The rubric is code — versioned and testable.
NO MODELTOOL: 7 KIQs rubric
Guard
Fact-checker
Kiểm chứng dữ kiện — Gate L1
@gate-L1
“Letter-for-letter, or it doesn't pass.”
Rejects any name, date or number not found verbatim in the cited evidence — checked independently in Vietnamese and English. Free, instant, reproducible.
NO MODELGATE: exact match
Guard
Independent Verifier
Kiểm chứng độc lập — Gate L2
@gate-L2
“Nobody grades their own homework.”
A second, unrelated AI re-checks entailment of every insight — the app refuses to boot if writer and verifier share a model family.
MODELINVARIANT: ≠ writer family
+ 1 Human — the signature · Chữ ký con người
A Product SME reviews every edition against a written checklist, with direct citation spot-checks, before anything publishes. And when the evidence is too thin, the report says INSUFFICIENT_EVIDENCE instead of filling the page.
